    Enrolment Process

    Application
    An Application for Admission form may be lodged at any time, but making an early application increases the likelihood of securing a place.

    Application Fee
    The application fee is to accompany this Application for Admission form. The current Fees and Charges information is available on the school website under Enrolments. The registration fee is non-refundable.

    Next Steps

    The first stage of the enrolment process involves the completion of the Application for Admission form, accompanied by the Birth Certificate and Proof of Residency (if applicable)

    Siblings do not automatically gain entry to the school; this procedure must be followed for each child in the family wishing to attend

    Approximately 18 months (for boarders) and 12 months (for day students) prior to the year of entry, parents will be contacted by the school regarding the next stage of the enrolment process (which does include an interview).
